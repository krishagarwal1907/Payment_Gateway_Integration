Donate Now!
 Giving for animals is perhaps the most altruistic form of charity for its beneficiaries are unable to acknowledge or reciprocate your generosity. What you will receive though is the enormous satisfaction of knowing that you have done the most good for the most needy. As a non-profit organization, People for Animals, the flagship center of Sanjay Gandhi Animal Care Centre, is entirely dependent on public donations to sustain its vital work. All contributions are exempt from Income Tax under section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. We accept donations in cash or kind. Cheque or demand draft can be sent in favour of ‘People For Animals’. You can also donate by clicking on "DONATE" button.
